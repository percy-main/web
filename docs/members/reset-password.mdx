# Resetting Your Password

## If You've Forgotten Your Password

1. Go to [percymain.org/auth/login](https://percymain.org/auth/login)
2. Click **Forgot password?**
3. Enter the email address you registered with
4. Click **Submit** — we'll send you a password reset link
5. Check your email and click the reset link
6. Enter your new password and confirm it

You can now log in with your new password.

<Callout type="info">
If you don't receive the reset email, check your spam or junk folder. The link in the email expires after a period of time, so use it promptly.
</Callout>

## If You Know Your Current Password

You can change your password without a reset email:

1. Log in and go to [percymain.org/members](https://percymain.org/members)
2. Click the **Security** tab
3. Use the **Change Password** section to enter your current password and set a new one

## Two-Factor Authentication

If you have two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled, you'll need your authenticator app code after entering your password. If you've lost access to your authenticator app, you can use one of your recovery codes instead.

For more about security settings, see [Managing Your Account](account-management.mdx).
