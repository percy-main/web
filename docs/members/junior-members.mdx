# Junior Members

Parents and guardians can register their children for junior cricket through the website. You must have a verified account to register juniors.

## Before You Start

You'll need:

- A [signed-up](signing-up.mdx) account with a verified email address
- To be logged in

## Pricing

| | Cost |
|--|------|
| **First child** | £50 |
| **Each additional child** | £30 |

This is a one-off payment valid until the end of the calendar year. Junior membership does not auto-renew — you will need to register again the following year.

## Registration Process

Go to [percymain.org/membership/junior](https://percymain.org/membership/junior) to start. The registration has six steps:

### Step 1: Children's Information

Enter details for each child you want to register:

- Name, gender, date of birth, and school year
- You can add multiple children in one go

### Step 2: Cricket Experience

Tell us whether your child has played cricket before, and if so, where.

### Step 3: Contact Details

- Whether you consent to being added to WhatsApp team groups
- An alternative contact (e.g. a grandparent) and their WhatsApp consent

### Step 4: Medical Information

- GP surgery name and phone number
- Whether your child has a disability (and type, if applicable)
- Any medical conditions we should know about
- **Emergency medical consent** — this is mandatory and allows us to seek medical treatment in an emergency
- **Medical fitness declaration** — also mandatory

<Callout type="warning">
The emergency medical consent and medical fitness declaration are both required. Registration cannot proceed without them.
</Callout>

### Step 5: Consents

- **Data protection / GDPR consent** — how we handle your child's information
- **Photo and video consent** — whether we can use photos/videos of your child for club purposes

### Step 6: Review and Pay

Review all the details you've entered and check the total cost. When you click **Confirm & Pay**, the registration is submitted and a charge is created on your account.

You'll then be redirected to the **Payments** tab in the Members Area where you can complete the payment.

## After Registration

Once registered and paid, your children will appear in your account. Club coaches and junior managers will be in touch about training sessions and match schedules.
