# How to Pay Membership

Once you've [signed up](signing-up.mdx) and verified your email, you can pay for your membership.

## Membership Categories

Go to [percymain.org/membership/pay](https://percymain.org/membership/pay) and choose your membership category:

| Category | Who it's for |
|----------|-------------|
| **Senior Player** | Adult players |
| **Social** | Supporters and friends of the club |
| **Student / Concessionary** | Students and concessions (reduced rate) |
| **Women's Player** | Women's team members |

## Payment Schedule

All categories offer two payment options:

- **Annual** (or **Season** for Women's Player) — a one-off payment
- **Monthly** — a recurring subscription that renews each month

## Making Payment

1. Select your membership category
2. Choose your payment schedule (annual or monthly)
3. Enter your card details — payment is handled securely through Stripe
4. Click **Pay**

Your membership is active immediately once payment is confirmed.

## Viewing Your Payments

You can view your payment history at any time:

1. Go to [percymain.org/members](https://percymain.org/members)
2. Click the **Payments** tab

This shows:

- **One-off payments** — annual membership fees, junior registrations, donations
- **Subscriptions** — monthly payments and their current status

<Callout type="info">
If you need to change your membership category or have a question about a payment, contact the club committee.
</Callout>
