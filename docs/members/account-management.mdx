# Managing Your Account

The Members Area at [percymain.org/members](https://percymain.org/members) is your hub for managing your membership. It has four tabs.

## Membership Tab

View your current membership status and category.

## Details Tab

Update your personal information:

- Name, title, and address
- Telephone number
- Emergency contact name and phone number

Keep these up to date so we can reach you when needed.

## Security Tab

### Change Password

Enter your current password and choose a new one. Use this if you want to update your password while logged in — no reset email needed.

### Passkeys

Set up passkeys for faster, password-free login. Passkeys use your device's biometrics (fingerprint, Face ID) or security key to authenticate you.

To add a passkey:

1. Click **Add Passkey** in the Security tab
2. Follow your browser's prompts to register the passkey
3. Next time you log in, you can use the passkey instead of typing your password

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your account using a time-based one-time password (TOTP).

To enable 2FA:

1. Click **Enable 2FA** in the Security tab
2. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (e.g. Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password)
3. Enter the 6-digit code from the app to confirm

<Callout type="info">
When you enable 2FA, you'll receive recovery codes. Save these somewhere safe — they're the only way to access your account if you lose your authenticator device.
</Callout>

## Payments Tab

View your complete payment history:

- **One-off payments** — annual membership fees, junior registrations, donations, and any charges added by an admin
- **Subscriptions** — monthly membership payments with their current status
