# How to Sign Up

Joining Percy Main Community Sports Club is a three-step process: fill in your details, create an account, and verify your email.

## Step 1: Fill in Your Details

Go to [percymain.org/membership/join](https://percymain.org/membership/join) and complete the membership form.

You'll need to provide:

- **Title and name**
- **Address** — you can search by postcode to find your address quickly
- **Date of birth**
- **Telephone number**
- **Email address** — this will become your login email
- **Emergency contact** — a name and phone number for someone we can contact in an emergency

Once you've filled everything in, click **Submit**.

## Step 2: Create Your Account

After submitting the form, you'll be taken to a registration page where you set a password. Your email address will be pre-filled from the previous step.

Choose a strong password and click **Register**.

## Step 3: Verify Your Email

We'll send a verification email to the address you provided. Click the link in the email to confirm your account.

<Callout type="info">
If you don't see the verification email, check your spam or junk folder. The email comes from percymain.org.
</Callout>

Once your email is verified, you can log in and [pay your membership](paying-membership.mdx).

## What Happens Next?

After signing up and verifying your email, you'll want to:

1. **[Pay your membership](paying-membership.mdx)** — choose a membership category and payment schedule
2. **[Register junior members](junior-members.mdx)** — if you have children who want to play (requires a verified account)
