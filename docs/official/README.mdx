# Official Guide

This guide covers the Match Official panel — a tool for managing matchday squads, fees, and expenses.

## Who is this for?

This documentation is for club members with the <RoleBadge role="official" /> role. Officials are assigned to specific teams and manage matchday operations for those teams.

<RoleBadge role="admin" /> admins also have full access to the official panel for all teams.

## Accessing the Official Panel

1. Log in to the website at [percymain.org](https://percymain.org)
2. Navigate to `/official`
3. You must have the <RoleBadge role="official" /> or <RoleBadge role="admin" /> role — regular members are redirected to the members area

If you see "No teams assigned", contact an admin to be assigned as an official for your team(s).

## Matchday Lifecycle

Every matchday follows three stages:

| Stage | What happens |
|-------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Squad is being assembled — add/remove players freely |
| **Confirmed** | Team is locked in — match fees generated, payment tracking active |
| **Finished** | Match complete — unpaid fees remain as charges, notification emails sent |

Once a matchday moves to the next stage, it cannot go back.

## Step-by-step: Managing a Matchday

### 1. Select your team

The official panel shows cards for each team you're assigned to. Click a team to see its upcoming matches (pulled from Play-Cricket).

### 2. Create a matchday

For each upcoming match, click **Select Team** to create a matchday record. This links the match to your squad management.

If a matchday already exists, you'll see **Edit Squad** (pending), **View Confirmed** (confirmed), or **View Finished** (finished) instead.

### 3. Build the squad

While the matchday is in the **pending** stage:

1. Click **Add Player** to open the player search
2. Type at least 2 characters to search club members by name
3. Click a member to add them to the squad
4. To add someone not in the system, use the "Or add a player not in the system" field and click **Add**
5. To remove a player, click **Remove** next to their name

Players are numbered in the order they're added.

### 4. Confirm the team

When the squad is ready:

1. Click **Confirm Team**
2. Set each player's status using the dropdown:
   - **Playing** — the player is playing and will be charged a match fee
   - **Dropped Out** — the player dropped out before the match (no charge)
   - **No Show** — the player didn't turn up (no charge)
3. Click **Confirm Team** to lock it in

<Callout type="warning">
Confirming the team generates match fee charges for all players marked as **Playing**. This action cannot be undone.
</Callout>

### 5. Track match fee payments

After confirmation, each playing player shows a fee status badge:

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Fee paid** (green) | Payment received |
| **Fee pending** (yellow) | Charge created, awaiting payment |
| **No fee set** (grey) | No fee rate configured for this player/team combination |

To record a payment:

1. Click **Mark Paid** next to the player
2. Choose the payment method: **Cash**, **Bank Transfer**, or **Card**

### 6. Record expenses

After the team is confirmed, the **Expenses** section appears. You can add:

| Expense type | Description field |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Umpire Fee** | Umpire name (e.g. "J. Smith") |
| **Scorer Fee** | Scorer name (e.g. "A. Jones") |
| **Match Ball** | Tick "New match ball used" and enter cost |
| **Teas** | Description (optional) |
| **Miscellaneous** | Description |

To add an expense:

1. Click **Add Expense**
2. Select the expense type
3. Fill in the description and amount
4. Click **Add**

To remove an expense, click **Remove** next to it. Expenses cannot be removed after the match is finished.

<Callout type="info">
Only one match ball expense can be added per matchday. If one already exists, the option is hidden.
</Callout>

### 7. Finish the match

When all payments and expenses are recorded:

1. Click **Finish Match**
2. Read the confirmation — unpaid match fees will remain as charges and notification emails will be sent to members with outstanding fees

<Callout type="warning">
Finishing a match is permanent. Make sure all cash/bank transfer payments have been recorded first, as the match cannot be reopened.
</Callout>

After finishing, the matchday shows the completion date and switches to read-only mode. Expenses are still visible but cannot be modified.
